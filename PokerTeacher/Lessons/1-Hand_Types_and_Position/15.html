Hand Types and Position

POSITION + HAND TYPE STRATEGY:

PREMIUM HANDS (AA, KK, AK)

  • Play from any position
  • Raise aggressively

SPECULATIVE HANDS (87, 55, J10)

  • Need good position
  • Can steal pots post-flop
  • Fold in early position

WEAK HANDS (K2, Q7)

  • Fold most of the time
  • Even good position can't save them

STEALING HANDS (Any two vs. tight players)

  • Use position to attack blinds
  • Works against folding-prone opponents

KEY INSIGHT: Your position determines which hands are profitable. The button (late position) lets you play 2-3x more hands than early position!
