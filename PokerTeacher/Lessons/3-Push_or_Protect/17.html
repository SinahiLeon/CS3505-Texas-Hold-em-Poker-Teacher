PUSH VS. PROTECT DECISIONS:

When to VALUE BET (Push):

  • Safe, dry boards
  • Strong made hands
  • Against loose calling opponents
  • Bet 1/2 to 2/3 pot

When to PROTECT BET (Push Hard):

  • Dangerous, wet boards
  • Vulnerable strong hands
  • Against drawing hands
  • Bet 2/3 to full pot

When to POT CONTROL (Protect):

  • Medium-strength hands
  • Multi-way dangerous pots
  • Against aggressive opponents
  • Check-call to keep pots small

Quick flow:

1. How strong is my hand?

2. How dangerous is the board?

3. How many opponents?

4. Choose: Value • Protect • Control.
