The Bluffing Blueprint

FOLD EQUITY ESTIMATE:

  • What can they have that folds?

Weak Q and other card: 60 percent fold

Draws: 40 percent fold

Jacks: 50 percent fold

Pairs: 30 percent fold

Estimated fold equity: about 45 percent.

What happens if called?

  • You have about 10 percent chance to win
  • Risk: 15 dollars, Reward: 30 dollar pot
