BLUFFING CHECKLIST:

TELL A BELIEVABLE STORY

  • Does this board fit my pre-flop range?
  • What hand am I representing?
  • Does my betting pattern make sense?

CHOOSE THE RIGHT BOARDS

  • Good: A K Q, 9 8 7, flush-completing boards
  • Bad: 4 5 9, paired boards, dry low cards

SMART SIZING

  • Match your story with value-looking sizes
  • Consider the pot odds you are offering
  • Adjust to opponent tendencies

FOLD EQUITY CALC

  • Estimate what percent of the time they fold
  • More than 33 percent fold is usually profitable
  • Consider their player type (tight or loose)

QUICK FLOW:

1. Can I tell a good story?

2. Does the board favor my range?

3. What is their fold percent?

4. Choose sizing that tells my story.
