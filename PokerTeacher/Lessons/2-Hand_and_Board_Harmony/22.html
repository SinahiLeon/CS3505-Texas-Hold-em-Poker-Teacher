Hand & Board Harmony

HAND + BOARD MATCHUPS:

PREMIUM PAIRS (AA, KK, QQ)

  • Love DRY boards (K-8-2, Q-7-3)
  • Bet for value - few scare cards

DRAWING HANDS (87s, JTs, 65s)

  • Need WET boards (J-T-4, 9-8-7)
  • Look for straight/flush possibilities
  • Fold on dry boards without good draws

MEDIUM STRENGTH

  • Dry boards = value bet medium
  • Wet boards = bet large for protection

QUICK TIP: Before betting, ask: “Does this board favor my hand type?”
