DRAW MATHEMATICS:

RULE OF 2 and 4:

  • Turn only: Outs × 2 = percent to hit
  • Turn and River: Outs × 4 = percent to hit
  • Flush: 9 outs = 18 percent / 36 percent
  • OESD: 8 outs = 16 percent / 32 percent

POT ODDS CALCULATION:

Call Amount : Total Pot After Call

Example: Call 10 to win 30 = 1:3 = 25 percent

WHEN TO CALL:

  • Immediate Pot Odds greater than Drawing Odds
  • OR good Implied Odds (deep stacks, hidden draws)

SEMI-BLUFFING:

  • Good equity if called
  • Fold equity if bet
  • Position and initiative

QUICK REFERENCE:

  • Flush draw: Need 4:1 (1 card)
  • Straight draw: Need 5:1 (1 card)
  • Gutshot: Need 11:1 (1 card)
