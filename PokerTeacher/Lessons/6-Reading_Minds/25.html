THINKING LEVELS:

LEVEL 1: My cards

  • What hand do I have?
  • How strong is it?

LEVEL 2: Their cards

  • What kind of range do they have?
  • What kind of player are they?

LEVEL 3: Their perception

  • What do they think I have?
  • What kind of story am I telling?

PLAYER TYPE EXPLOITATION:

CALLING STATIONS USUALLY:

  • Value bet small
  • Rarely bluff
  • Extract minimum possible value

MANIACS USUALLY:

  • Call and bet often
  • Bluff much more
  • Do not fold easily

NITS USUALLY:

  • Fold to aggression
  • Are easily affected by bluffs
  • Only play strong hands

Quick tip: Identify player type in the first few rounds and then play accordingly. Change up your own strategy to be unpredictable while still making safe moves.
